## About Asyntra

**AS Consulting Services** is a SaaS Company that automates revenue cycle and billing workflows for healthcare providers and billing teams. We help organizations reduce claim errors, accelerate collections, and improve patient billing experiences by combining automation, analytics, and secure integrations.

## 🔧 Key Features

- **Automated Billing Workflows** — Reduce manual errors and speed up claims processing
- **Claims & Eligibility Checks** — Validate and track claims end-to-end
- **Billing Support & Collections** — Tools for retries, reconciliation, and follow-ups
- **Patient Invoicing & Payment Links** — Simplified patient communications and online payments
- **Analytics & Reporting** — Actionable dashboards for AR, denial rates, and revenue forecasts
- **Integrations** — Connect to EHR/EMR systems, payment gateways, and third-party billing tools
- **API-first** — Full-featured API and SDK for custom automation and platform integrations
- **Security & Compliance** — Designed with HIPAA and SOC-2 principles in mind

## 🏥 Industries We Serve

- **Healthcare Providers** (hospitals, clinics, private practices, telehealth)
- **Medical Billing Companies** (outsourced billing & revenue cycle management)
- **Diagnostic & Imaging Centers**
- **Insurance & Payer Support Teams**
- **Any organization needing robust billing support and automation**

## ✅ Benefits

- Faster claims turnaround and fewer denials
- Improved cash flow and reduced days in AR
- Lower operational costs through automation
- Better patient satisfaction with transparent billing

## 📦 Use Cases

- Automate eligibility checks and reduce rejected claims
- Centralize medical billing for multi-location clinics
- Provide secure patient invoicing and online payment options
- Generate financial reports for leadership and compliance audits
